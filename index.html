The Perfect Media Janitor Welcome To The Solution

Maintainerr makes managing your media easy. No longer will you have to worry about your precious hard drive space being taken up by media, that isn't even being watched.

The Best Way to Manage Your Library

Maintainerr helps you find those outlying media items that have just been sitting there in your library. If you use Overseerr, it is easy for your users to start downloading everything they can think of. Sometimes, they don't even watch it.

Utilize multiple parameters across Plex, Sonarr, Radarr, Tautulli, and Overseerr to find unwatched/unwanted media, and get rid of it.

A library management system built to help you save space.

Maintainerr was created to assist you in freeing up storage space. Here are some of its useful features :

Collection Management

Make Collections from a specific set of rules, defined by you

Plex Overview

See an overview of your Plex library contents

Manual Control

Manually add media items into or remove media from a collection

Plex Collections

Show your new collection on the Plex Home screen

Countdown Timer

Set a number of days the collection will exist before its contents are deleted

*arr Integration

Overseerr Integration

Storage Management

Tautulli Integration

Use information and statistics from your Tautulli installation.

Frequently Asked Questions
Will Maintainerr accidentally delete all of my media?

The minimum amount of time that "days before removal" will function, is 1 day. At a minimum you will have one day to check before anything is removed.

Can I use multiple Radarr/Sonarr servers?

Currently, we only support one instance. There are plans to expand this functionality, but it has not been implemented yet.

Does Maintainerr have authentication?

Currently, there is no authentication setup. This could be a possible future feature, but for now it is strongly encouraged that you use an external service. Authelia and Authentik are two great options.

Sound like the solution?

Maintainerr is easy to get up and running with Docker. You can find instructions and tutorials in our documentation. If you need help, feel free to reach out.
