Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This privacy policy explains how we use cookies and collect, store, and handle your information when you use our documentation website.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They are used to improve your browsing experience and provide insights into how our website is used.

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • To analyze user interactions with our documentation to identify areas for improvement.
  • To ensure that our website is functioning correctly and efficiently.
  • To remember user preferences, such as consent to analytics.
  • To track highly trafficked pages of the documentation.
  • To collect user feedback through the use of our feedback widgets. (only shown when analytics consent is given)

We Do Not Sell Your Information

We want to make it explicitly clear that we do not sell or share your information with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes. The data we collect through cookies is used exclusively to improve our documentation and enhance your experience.

Your Choices

You have full control over your cookie preferences. You can:

  • Accept cookies to help us analyze and improve our documentation.
  • Reject cookies to disable analytics and prevent the storage of non-essential cookies.

If you have previously accepted cookies, you can revoke your consent at any time through our cookie management settings.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We encourage you to review this page periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.
